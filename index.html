Happy Animal Sanctuary

Ellie the elephant

Ellie the elephant was a pretty radical animal. She often could be found riding her motorcycle and juggling tennis balls at the same time. She was also a competitive eater, what a pistol.

Foxxy the fox

Foxxy the fox was more of the torubled kind. He often was found picking fights with others around the santuary before he found his spirituality and commited to a life of silence.

Perry the parrot

Perry the parrot was a pillar of his community. he often volunteered with the ASPCA and developed a love for all animals. Hell of a chess player too. What a sweetie pie.

Tiggy the tiger

Terry the tiger had a gambling addiction. Thankfully, with the support of his friends and family he learned how to code and overcame his habit. He eventually ran for Mayor and was an all round cool guy.

Fred the dog

Fred the dog loved to chase his tail. He could often be found chasing a school bus and giving kids a bunch o' smooches. He lived to be a good boi and chase tennis balls until the cows came home.
